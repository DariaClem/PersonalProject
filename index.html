Dropbox Shell

The project is a shell that exposes the functionality of the dbxcli utility (ex. cp, mv, mkdir, rm) and connects with the local file system. I have enhanced the functionality of the dbxcli by adding the ability to upload and download entire folders, not just individual files. Additionally, I have implemented the "cd" command to make navigation through remote folders easier, a functionality that was not present in the original dbxcli.

Art projects

Drawing has been a significant passion of mine since I was young. It started with simple doodles and progressed into more complex works of art that capture my emotions and mood. I have always found joy in expressing myself through art, and it has become an essential part of my life.

During my formative years, I was fortunate enough to participate in a painting workshop in my hometown. This experience helped me hone my skills and learn what it's like to work in a professional studio. I was able to work with other artists and share techniques and ideas.

While I did not choose to pursue a career in art, painting remains an important hobby of mine. During my time in high school, I volunteered for the school magazine and created personalized drawings for various themes. Working with a team of people who held different roles and responsibilities was an enlightening experience. The hard work we put into the magazine paid off when we discovered that it was a national contest winner, which was a significant achievement for all of us.

In summary, drawing and painting have been a constant source of joy and inspiration in my life. Although I did not choose to pursue a career in art, I will always have a deep appreciation for the creativity and passion that it requires.

Blender projects

During high school, I stumbled upon 3D design by accident. I was completely fascinated when I discovered the Blender application and realized how many things you can create with it. I started with the tutorial, namely "Donut" made by Blender Guru. This represents the cornerstone for any 3D designer in Blender.

I quickly became consumed by the frenzy of creation and completed various projects that I reproduced with the help of tutorials. Later, I tried to create my own works. Although I have a long way to go, I can say that I am very excited about what the future holds for me and I can't wait to create new works.

In a few words, I can say that 3D design combines two of my passions: computer science and drawing, passions that I have described earlier.
